Services Selection Board - SSB

SSB practice lets you practice for Psycological tests in Services Selection Board (SSB) for Indian Army, Air Force and Navy.

Practice conditions will be similar to that of Services Selection Board (SSB). Practice tests are free and will be updated regularly.

Main goal is to practice these tests to remove the fear of Unknown before going for your Services Selection Board (SSB)

Currently, only Word Association Test (WAT) and Situation Reaction Test(SRT) is available . More tests will be added soon.
