Services Selection Board

SSB is the final stage of selection process for Indian Armed Forces. It is a 5-day long process which includes Group Tasks, Psychological Tests, Interview and Conference.

National Defence Academy(NDA)

Vacancies Per Course

For Men -320 (twice a year)
Army - 208
Navy - 42
Air Force - 70
For Women - as per Notification

Age Eligibility

16 ½ to 19½ yrs as on first day of the month in which course is due to commence

Qualification

12th Class for Army
12th Class with Physics and Maths for Air Force and Navy

Marital Status

Unmarried

Selection Process

Written Exam - conducted by UPSC
Services Selection Board

Duration

3 Yrs at NDA 1 Yr at IMA

Apply Online on UPSC Website as per dates given in the Notfication in the months of Jun and Dec.

10+2 Technical Entry Scheme (TES)

Vacancies Per Course

For Men -90 (twice a year)

Age Eligibility

16 ½ to 19½ yrs as on first day of the month in which course is due to commence

Qualification

12th PCM - Aggregate 60%+ and appeared in JEE(Mains) for B.E/B.Tech

Marital Status

Unmarried

Duration

5 Yrs (1 Yrs OTA GAYA & 4 Yrs CTWS) Permanent Commission after 4 Yrs. Phase-I- 01 Year Pre-commissioning Training at OTA Gaya & 03 Years at CME Pune/MCTE Mhow/MCEME Secunderabad Phase-II- 01 Year Post commission training at CME Pune/MCTE Mhow/MCEME Secunderabad

How to apply

Apply online on Official website of DG Rtg www.joinindianarmy.nic.in as Notified in the Notification.

Commencement of training

Jan and Jul
