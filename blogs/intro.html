The purpose of this blog is to timely update candidates about the latest trends that are being followed in SSBs, the type of tests that are being conducted and also how to approach these tests and how to Prepare yourself to go and conquer your next Services Selection Board.

Another purpose of this blog is to update candidates of the latest Current Affairs, latest developments in the Indian Armed Forces and also to motivate them with some real life Indian Army stories.




Services Selection Board’s 5 day process to assess a candidate is one of the finest in the world. In these 5 days a candidate’s mental make up and physical capabilities are very well assessed by the assessors, to declare them recommended or not recommended for the Indian Army. For more information about the SSB procedure follow the link: About SSB

Every years lakhs of candidates apply to join the Indian Armed Forces through various entries, and only close to 1000 finally reach the various academies. This extremely low selection rate suggests that the SSB's primary objective is quality over quantity. Every Candidate recommended is a quality candidate. As the margin of error in the armed forces is zero, therefore, there can be no compromise on quality of the Officers.

To achieve that quality one has to actually put in conscious effort to lead a well disciplined and purpose driven Life. One cannot change themselves over night, we have to regularly work on ourselves in order to improve. SSB Practice offers you regular practice of SSB tests – for free. Practice makes a person perfect and that’s what we strive for at SSB Practice.

Keep following our blog and SSB Practice on Instagram for regular updates and content. You can access SSB Practice tests from the SSB Practice website.
