Instructions - TAT
    Prerequisites
  • 6 sheets of paper
  • Pen
  • 1 hour of time
    • Instructions
  • 12 images will be displayed on the screen.
  • An image will be flashed for 30 seconds
  • You have to carefully observe the picture for 30 seconds
  • After 30 seconds,you will be given 4 minutes to write a story about the image.
  • After 4 minutes the next picture will be flashed
  • 11 pictures will have a scene and the 12th image will be blank, you have to write a story from your imagination.
  • After completion of the test go through your answers and take your time to think if you could have made a better story and write it down next to it.
  • After completion of the test, you can reattempt the test, or practice other tests.
