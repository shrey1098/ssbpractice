Instructions - WAT
    Prerequisites
  • Two sheets of paper
  • Pen
  • 15 minutes time
    • Instructions
  • 60 words will be flashed on the screen one after the other.
  • Each word will be flashed for 15 seconds
  • You have the see the word and write down the first sentence that comes to your mind in 15 seconds.
  • After 15 seconds, the next word will be flashed.
  • Do this for all 60 words.
  • Try to attempt as many as possible.
  • After completion of the test go through your answers and take your time to think if you could have made a better sentence and write it down next to it.
  • After completion of the test, you can reattempt the test, or practice other tests.

Test Finished

Thank you for completing the test.
